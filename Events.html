Events

August 2024

August 10: Falcon 9 | Starlink 8-3

o Mission: Launch of Starlink V2 Mini satellites. o Location: Cape Canaveral, Florida (Spaceflight Now).

August 11:

o Electron | Acadia 3: Rocket Lab will launch the Acadia 3 satellite from New Zealand, a mission named "A Sky Full Of SARs" (Spaceflight Now). o Falcon 9 | Arctic Satellite Broadband Mission (ASBM): SpaceX will launch a mission focused on improving Arctic region communications (Spaceflight Now).

August 13: Perseid Meteor Shower Peak

o Natural Event: One of the best meteor showers of the year, the Perseids will peak on this night, offering a dazzling display with up to 100 meteors per hour in ideal conditions (Sky & Telescope).

September 2024

September 5: BepiColombo Mercury Flyby

o Mission: ESA’s BepiColombo completes its fourth flyby of Mercury, setting the stage for orbital insertion in 2025 (Sky & Telescope).

September 24: Falcon 9 | Crew-9 Mission

o Mission: SpaceX will send four astronauts to the ISS, continuing international collaboration in space exploration (Spaceflight Now). • September 29: Harvest Moon

October 2024

October 5: BepiColombo Mercury Flyby

o Mission: Fifth flyby of Mercury by ESA’s BepiColombo (Sky & Telescope).

• October 14: Annular Solar Eclipse

o Natural Event: An annular solar eclipse, where the Moon covers the center of the Sun leaving a "ring of fire," will be visible across parts of North and South America (Sky & Telescope).

October (TBD): Artemis II Preparations

o Mission: Key preparations for NASA’s Artemis II, the mission that will carry astronauts around the Moon (Sky & Telescope).
